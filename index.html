ABOUT
NSS IIT Roorkee, under the aegis of the Dean of Students Welfare, is a student-run fraternity that aims to foster the feelings of social service among the students and provides them with a platform where they can make a difference to the society. NSS IIT Roorkee has over 700 active members working rigorously on a plethora of humane activities like literacy drives, community healthcare, and women empowerment. They stay true to their motto ‘Not for me, but for the nation’ by organising blood donation camps, AIDS awareness drives, and other such noble events regularly. The aim of the organization is to produce socially responsible engineers and work for the all round development of the nation.
OUR MISSION
National Service Scheme is a government program designed to inculcate the idea of welfare in students. The objective was to ensure that the needy get help to enhance their standard of living and lead a life of dignity. With this goal in mind, NSS was established at an institutional level in IIT-Roorkee under the Dean of Student Welfare. In the spirit of social service, NSS-IITR works for environment conservation, literacy and community welfare - organising blood donation camps, eye check-ups, awareness drives about social issues, and much more.
